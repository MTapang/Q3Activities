DEIB LHOR ENRILE

a well-known captain of the basketball team of Benison International School. He is known for his various achievements and victories in basketball, yet behind all these bliss and fortunes, he was often challenged with the hardships of life everyday; having busy parents as a businessman and a doctor, and a brother who is in a coma because of a sudden accident. In spite of that, he met his one true love, the one who changed him to be a better version of himself, his "most valuable person", Maxpein Del Valle.

MAXPEIN DEL VALLE

an ordinary girl from Mindoro whose mother died at an early age and a father who had a different family. Fortunately, she had a loving grandmother and uncle raised her and dreamt of sending her to one of the most prestigious schools across the country along with the help of her father. However, despite being boy-ish, she suddenly met the man who would boil her blood each day from pulling off pranks to falling in love with him.

RANDALL ECHAVEZ

a man who visited Mindoro for a vacation, yet he met this simple girl that made him mesmerized by her beauty and simplicity. Believe it or not, they both fell in love, not until he left for the United States. He suddenly blocked and ghosted the girl he loved the most, and after years, he showed up along with his unsaid words and circumstances he faced during his time in the US.

KIM GOZON

if a king exists in the school, of course, there is a queen who thinks she owns the world. She is known to be the captain's ex-girlfriend. Indeed, ex-girlfriend because of a sudden cheating issue which led to the breakup of the couple. However, even after the breakup, she was still ambitious and hopeful to get back together with Deib.
